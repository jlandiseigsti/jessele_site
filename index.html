Jesse LE is a comedian/musician living in Pennsylvania. A veteran of both sketch and song-writing, LE blends music, pop culture, history, and broken expectations in his weird little brain. In 2019, Pittsburgh Magazine named him one of the "Five Funniest People You've Never Heard Of."
